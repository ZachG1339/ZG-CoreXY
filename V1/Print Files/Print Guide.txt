Print instructions:

(based on ender 3 pro performance)

All files will work on standard 0.2mm or less layer height with a 0.4mm nozzle or smaller

- NO SUPPORTS
- Print Speed: 25 - 70 mm/s 
- infill: 20-40%
- Walls: 1.2mm minimum
- Reccomended Material: PLA -> 215 C end temperature

Feel free to play with more settings however these were pragmatic for me
